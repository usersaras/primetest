Our Introduction

Prime Vendor Nepal is a division of American Business Network, Inc., a Business Process Management (BPM) company with offices in New York, and Vancouver, Canada. ABN provides business services for Application Innovation, Business Analytics, Business Strategy, Commerce Consulting, and Procurement and Logistics. Additionally, ABN provides managed outsourcing services in the area of Application Management, Global Process Services, IT Infrastructure Services, IT Outsourcing, and Staff Training.

ABN owns and operates Prime Vendor Inc., a procurement solution service company based in North Carolina. Its flagship product is e-procurement solution software, the most advance modular B2G (Business-to-Government) system. Prime Vendor Nepal leverages on the resources that have made e-procurement solution software successful.

PVN is a vital core division of ABN providing clients comprehensive IT expertise, design and development, IT and Business Process Outsourcing services management.
